About Us

Delanie opened her own Lemonade stand to help local charities. Each week, Cafe Delanie will have designated menu items. ALL sales of these designated items will be donated to a local charity/rescue. Each month we help a new charity. Please help Delanie give back to our community by purchasing items from this menu. Since she started her Lemonade stand in April 2019, you have helped raise and donate over $100,000 to local animal charities/rescues. She thanks all of you for your help and support
